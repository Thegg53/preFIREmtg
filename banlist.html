preFIRE Modern Banlist

The goal is that as more matches are played and the format matures the Banned List will grow while the Watch List will shrink. We are also not discarding the possibility of other cards not listed here finding their way into either the Banned or Watch lists. The main objective is for both the Banned and Watch list to be as small as possible in order to allow players the most possibilities and to allow the largest amount of viable decks.

PreFIRE Modern Watchlist

We have cards that aren't banned in Modern, but are still on the watch list. Depending on how the format plays out some of these could prove to be too good.
